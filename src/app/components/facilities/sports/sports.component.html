Sports and Extracurricular Activities

The college prioritizes sports and extracurricular activities alongside academics. It offers extensive sports facilities including volleyball, basketball, badminton, football, and cricket. Evenings are lively with students engaging in various sports and enjoying as spectators. There's an on-campus basketball court for skill development and several sports clubs that actively involve students with faculty support.

In addition to sports, the college promotes diverse extracurricular activities. Cultural clubs like the anti-ragging cell, hobby club, literary club, IT club, NSS clubs, cultural clubs, and musical bands thrive with student participation. It also hosts developmental cells such as the Women’s Development Cell and various academic clubs. The campus boasts a fully equipped fitness zone, encouraging students to maintain their health with guidance from trainers.
