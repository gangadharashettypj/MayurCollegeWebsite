CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE

The Bapujee Education Society was established in 1969 with the mission to provide excellent education infused with life values. The campus is situated in the heart of the city and spans a wide area. It is adorned with photos depicting the life history of Mahatma Gandhi, the social reformer and leader of the Indian independence movement, who introduced the concept of non- violent resistance known as Satyagraha. These images engage students in learning about Mahatma Gandhiâ€™s life and values. Since its inception, the education society has grown in size and stature, adding new courses and impressive infrastructure facilities. Mayur College of Pharmacy, established in 2006, is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bangalore, approved by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), and recognized by the Government of Karnataka (GOK). This college is among the best institutions for imparting quality education while incorporating contemporary developments.
I am pleased to announce that our institute's website has been revamped, now offering substantial information for users both on and off-campus. The information provided is authenticated to assure our stakeholders, facilitating their decision to send their wards to study here. We have upgraded the website in all respects to assess its worthiness and ensure it receives due consideration from all.
In today's competitive and privatized world of professional education, it is imperative to provide necessary and essential information, and I am confident we have succeeded in doing so through this website. I convey my best wishes to everyone through this platform, hoping it will enable more frequent and closer communication with us. We also hope that our alumni, who are spread across premier organizations in India and abroad, will access this site and see how their alma mater has grown, continually maintaining its academic excellence and fostering true leadership qualities.

Mr. M. Basavaiah.
Chairman,
Bapujee Education society,
Tumkur, Karnataka, India.

Vision Mission
To provide the students with knowledge, skills, values and sensitivity to face the challenges in life both in academic field as well as in their personal life. To develop the human potentials to achieve different opportunities in future. To enhance the new teaching learning process for students, faculties and staffs. To become a "Centre of excellence" by providing quality and research oriented Pharmacy education to meet the needs of the industry, community and other stake holders through continuous training and up-gradation of infrastructure of learning and practicing Pharmacy profession.
