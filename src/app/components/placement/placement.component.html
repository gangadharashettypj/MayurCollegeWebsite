Placements

100% placement guaranteed after successful completion of the course.

Our alumni are open to choosing a career in various reputed hospitals and healthcare centers. Our first priority for recruitment is always our students, and we ensure they receive the best annual package as per market standards when they start their careers. Our institution also has a placement center that provides information, guidance, and support for placements in other reputed hospitals and healthcare centers. It conducts regular training, tests, and pre-placement advisory services, acting as a connecting link between our students and various other reputed healthcare institutions, guiding them in building a successful career and future.

Most of our alumni are currently pursuing their careers in well-reputed hospitals, healthcare centers, and institutions in India and abroad.
